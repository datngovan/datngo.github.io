My Projects

A upgraded chatbox for Foody platform

Summary:

My project is a chatbox for Foody platforms. That will release some messages automatically when the drivers accept the offers of customers (like Grab and GoViet). This chatbox shows the status of the request, such as, "The drivers arrive at the restaurant."That is similar to another chatbox we use that have two people connect in a chatbox to communicate directly.

Motivation:

Foody is the very first company that gets into the food delivery area. However, the Foody app doesn't have a good chatbox. Customers have to chat with support service staff first. That leads to a distraction when the information comes to the drivers. I am used to a Foody user because of Foody chat box is very terrible. You have to text and wait for the staff's answer. Then the information will be sent to the driver's phone. Those things lead to the increase in delivery time compared with their competitive platforms: Grab and Goviet.

Description:

There are three primary functions that this chatbox will have. First of all, I will reduce the role of the support service staff position in the chatbox because it is a waste of time and productivity. Instead of that, I will allow customers to change their address if wrong and add the delivery after. This new chatbox will help Foody to cut off a lot of unnecessary employees and reduce the delivery cost.

Secondly, I will add chatbots that help customers to change the address as well as answer the FAQ questions. If the chat box cannot help, they can chat directly with the support services staff. Which allows customers to have more control with the system. Because we don't want to talk will a team when we do not need help. The FAQ is used to make analysts about customers' behavior individually. Therefore, Foody can use that to specialize in what types of food customers prefer. Moreover, the chatbot can work 24/7.

Finally, adding the call button. That allows customers and drivers to contact each other via phone numbers previously registered. This function is beneficial for drivers who want to confirm the request directly and contact if problems such as: out of stock, restaurants closed, and price changes. For customers, they can instruct the driver or be notified when the dish arrives directly.

However, there are some drawbacks to this chatbox. The following paragraphs will point out those problems and how can we solve them. Firstly, the chatbots maybe not effective immediately due to the undeveloped. However, we can fix it automatically by collecting customers using behavior, for example, adding more FQAs by popular answer questions recently.

Secondly, the support service staff number is reducing while chatbot develops. But we still need some support service staff. Because people sometimes struggle with apps, and they need some live chat. I call it "al Time Convenience to Customers" "which will provide the customers' believe that they can have help whenever they want. So a support service staff available in working hours is necessary.

Tools and Technologies:

I have to create a chatbot use DialogFlow and offers integration with Google Assistant, Facebook, Telegrame. Also, I will create a backend app for the bot using Node.js able to handle user requests and actions of customers.

Skills Required:

I will code software for the call button by using Python. Create a Node.js back-end app to handle customer requests. Also, I will use Google Assistant to develop AI chatterbot by adding more popular FAQs, and a physical server has to be installed because the AI chatterbot and chatbox have to process a large amount of data

Outcome:

The original chatbox of Foody is a chatbox for at least three people: customer, support service staff and driver. The support service staff confirm the offers and ask customers if the address is right. However, if the wrong address customer cannot change by themselves, they have to send a request to the staff. This process is too cumbersome. It makes customers and drivers passive, leading to an extension of delivery time, usually about 30 minutes longer than Grab as well as the increase in delivery price. If this project is applied, the process will be significantly reduced. Foody does not need to hire too many collaborators for support work and focus on branding, discounts to counter other platforms.
